Task 1 (NumPy + Pandas + Matplotlib): Time Series Sales Analysis
You have sales data for 2 years (730 days). Your task is to:

Generate a NumPy array of 730 random numbers (daily sales, ranging from 10 to 200).
Create a DataFrame, where:
The first column is dates (from "2022-01-01" to "2023-12-31").
The second column is sales.
Add a column with a 30-day moving average.
Plot a line chart of sales, including the moving average line.
Determine the month with the highest average sales.
